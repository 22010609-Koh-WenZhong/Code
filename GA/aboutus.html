Local Transfer

Local bank transfer is a process of transferring funds electronically from one bank account to another within the same nation or area. It enables people, corporations, and organizations to transfer funds to receivers who have accounts with different banks in the same geographic region.

Overseas Transfer

Overseas transfer is an online transfer of funds from one bank account in one nation to another bank account in a different country. Individuals, corporations, and organizations can use it to make international payments or transmit money across borders..
